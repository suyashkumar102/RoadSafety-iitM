# Road Safety Hackathon 2026

## Organizer
- Centre of Excellence for Road Safety (CoERS), RBG Labs, IIT Madras

## Theme
- AI in Road Safety (with focus on AI-powered chatbots)

## Team Size
- 1 to 10 members

## Problem Statements
- DriveLegal
- RoadWatch
- RoadSoS

### DriveLegal
- **Short description:** A location-aware chatbot for traffic laws, violations, and fine schedules.
- **Key aspects:**
  - Geo-fenced lookup of local traffic compounding fees
  - Automated challan calculator (by violation and vehicle type)
  - Global applicability
  - Offline support for low-network conditions
- **Evaluation criteria:**
  - Legal accuracy and regulatory coverage
  - Challan calculator correctness
  - Cross-region information integration
  - UI and accessibility

### RoadWatch
- **Short description:** A chatbot to monitor road quality, track public spending, and route civic complaints.
- **Key aspects:**
  - Road type, relaying date, contractor details
  - Complaint routing to relevant authority
  - Budget sanctioned vs spent
  - Global applicability
  - Offline support for low-network conditions
- **Evaluation criteria:**
  - Data accuracy
  - Complaint routing quality
  - Budget transparency
  - UI and accessibility
  - Cross-region information integration

### RoadSoS
- **Short description:** A location-based emergency support tool for accidents.
- **Key aspects:**
  - Nearest police stations, hospitals, ambulance services
  - Towing, puncture shops, showrooms
  - Global applicability
  - Offline support for low-network conditions
- **Evaluation criteria:**
  - Reliability and data accuracy
  - Number and quality of fetched contacts
  - Offline functionality
  - Innovation and extra features
  - Cross-region information integration
